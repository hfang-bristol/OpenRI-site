Section 4 Mining - Browse

FIGURE 4.1: The context browse interfaces for fetching regulatory interactions via navigating contexts, illustrated for the context C0001: A549_Roadmap (PMID33828297). The integers in the red hexagons denote sequential clicks. Top: the browse page lists contexts for regulatory interactions with different modals, including the modal Activity-By-Contact (ABC). Bottom-left: the context-specific page for the context C0001: A549_Roadmap (PMID33828297), which lists its regulatory interactions between genomic regions and genes (each gene hyperlinked to the gene-specific page). Bottom-right: the gene-specific page for the gene ROCK1P1, which lists its regulatory interactions separately for different modals (this is, ABC, PCHiC and QTL), with each context hyperlinked to the context-specific page.
